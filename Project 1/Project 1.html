Friends of Farmers
Our Lands, Our Pride

We belong to the proud lands of Gir where farming is worshipped. Where food is grown not to feed, but to nurture. But lately, our lands are suffering as the modern farming practices wreak their havoc on our soil.

Once fertile, these rich lands are turning barren with each passing day. So, we decided not to sit back and watch..

How is Girveda Helping?

All our products are grown using Cow-Based Natural Farming (CBNF) and NO CHEMICAL FERTILISERS OR PESTICIDES!
From using cow-dung and urine as manure and natural pest repellent to managing tasks like plowing and threshing grains, farmers have access to everything they need for cultivating land with the help of cows.

Friends of Farmers

Being a Direct to Customer Brand, we help the farmers get the best value for their produce by providing them a direct market and helping them to reach more customers. This encourages them to practice CBNF and which in turn doesn't just keep us, but also our soil healthy.
